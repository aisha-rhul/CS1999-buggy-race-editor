This website was created as part of the RHUL coursework for CS1999. The idea is to create a virtual racing buggy according to the rules specified. There are several options that you can choose to build a buggy. However, many options will add add to the cost of the buggy and will affect its racing performance. Each buggy design parameters can be converted to JSON format for entry into the race. The final design can be submitted by clicking here.

Remember that if your data is not accepted by the race server, your buggy will be disqualified from that race.
