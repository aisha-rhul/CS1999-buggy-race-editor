What is the buggy race editor?

The buggy race editor is a website that allows you to create your own virtual buggy!

There are several options to choose from to ensure that your buggy has the best specification and is ready to race.

What's great about the buggy race editor?

This editor will let you create a customised buggy of your choice!

When customising your buggy, there are certain rules that you must follow in order to submit your buggy to the race

These rules are listed on the home page. Be sure to follow these rues or you will not be allowed to enter the race!

What can you do with it?

You can race your customised buggy with other users. This can be done by clicking here.

There will be a race every night and results will be displayed once the race has finished.

What features does it have?

The features when creating your buggy include:

  • Number of wheels
  • A primary motive power
  • Units for your primary motive power
  • A secondary motive power (in case your primary runs out)
  • Units for your secondary motive power
  • Flag colours and patterns (this flag is generated for your customised buggy!)
  • Type of your tyres
  • Number of tyres (in case one breaks down)
  • Armour for your buggy
  • The type of attack
  • The number of attacks
  • If you would like your buggy to be fireproof
  • If you would like your buggy to be insulated
  • If you would like your buggy to have antibiotic enabled
  • If you would like your buggy to have banging enabled
  • And of course, the algorithm that your buggy will have when racing!

After choosing your buggy specification, a total cost will then be displayed to you.

You may also choose a default buggy or a random buggy if you would like.
